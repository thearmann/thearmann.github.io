Classpass mobile app

ClassPass is a monthly fitness membership offering access to the highest quality boutique fitness studios in major cities. Working along side two other designers, my responsibilities included UX and visual design for this mobile app. We aimed to create a visually pleasing and intuitive reservation system that would allow our members to sign up for their classes on the go, as well as creating a backlog of their fitness history and favorite classes.
